Save the date: Python in Astronomy 2016

Week of 21-25 March 2016, University of Washington, Seattle



The second Python in Astronomy conference will be held during the week of 21-25 March 2016 (exact start/end day to be confirmed) at the University of Washington eScience Institute in Seattle, WA, UA. We are aiming for a similar format to the 2015 meeting, which was held at the Lorentz Center, with presentations, tutorials, unconference sessions, and coding sprints.

A formal website and registration process will be available soon, but in the meantime you can already sign up to receive any future announcements about the conference:


Please share this announcement with others! The hashtag for this meeting is #pyastro16
